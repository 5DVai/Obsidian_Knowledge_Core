## Key Concepts & Principles
- **Clarity:** Use explicit objectives, precise language, and logical structure.
- **Conciseness:** Focus content, avoid redundancy, and ensure token efficiency.
- **Consistency:** Define constraints, specify format, and ensure repeatable results.
- **Completeness:** Provide sufficient context, handle edge cases, and define success criteria.
- **Advanced Techniques:** Chain-of-thought prompting, few-shot learning, XML tagging, role-based prompting, and prompt chaining.
- **Multimodal Prompting:** Integration of vision, embeddings, and file-based prompts.
